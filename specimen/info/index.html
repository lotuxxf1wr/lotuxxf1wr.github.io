Avant Serif
Beauty , Fashion , Info

“Avant Garde Serif” is a type design project by Yuha Cho during Type Lovers lecture instructed by Ryan Budgen, Nick Sherman, and Marie Otsuka at School of Visual Arts.

This project was inspired by beauty, fashion, and other magazines from the avant-garde era. With Y2K trends making a comeback, modern people are returning to the past to commemorate and resurrect that era. From this concept, I’ve introduced Avant-Garde Serif, a paradoxical approach of adding serifs to the iconic sans-serif fonts from the past, bringing them back into the present.

This website is a type-focused platform created with skills taught by Marie Otsuka. Since Avant Garde Serif is a font designed for large readability, such as in newspaper headlines, it was paired with vintage photo books— the origin of the project— to add an element of fun.
